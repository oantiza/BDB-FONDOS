Objetivo de inversión
 
The investment objective of this Subfund is to
outperform the FTSE Europe Convertible index through
active management of convertible bonds. This objective
will be implemented via a managerial approach that
promotes the sustainability of issuers through an
analysis of the environmental, social and governance
(ESG) characteristics of the securities held in the
portfolio.
