Objetivo de inversión
 
The objective of the Fund is capital appreciation while
closely controlling risk. In addition, the Fund intends to
deliver absolute returns that have a low correlation with
equity and bond markets. The Fund aims to achieve its
investment objective by investing primarily in a
portfolio of equity securities of companies located
worldwide and listed or traded on Regulated Markets
and by pursuing the strategy detailed below. It is not
proposed to concentrate investments in any one
geographical region or sector.
