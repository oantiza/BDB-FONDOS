Objetivo de inversión
 
The fund aims to provide an absolute return over the
medium to long term. The fund invests at least 70% of
its assets, in equities of companies anywhere in the
world, including emerging markets, as well as cash and
money market instruments. In actively managing the
fund, the Investment Manager combines qualitative
and quantitative market analysis into a disciplined
portfolio construction process to allocate investments
across various securities.
