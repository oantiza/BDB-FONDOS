Objetivo de inversión
 
The Fund’s investment objective is to seek long term
appreciation, (measured in USD). The fund primarily
invests (at least 70% of total net assets) in equity
securities of issuers located in Asia, excluding Japan,
including depositary receipts (including American
Depositary Receipts (ADRs), Global Depositary Receipts
(GDRs)), and China A-shares (through Stock Connect).
The investment manager emphasises a bottom-up
stock selection process, seeking attractive investments
