Objetivo de inversión
 
The investment objective of the fund is to maximise total

actively managed bond portfolio. The Sub-Fund is
actively managed without reference to any benchmark.
The Sub-Fund is exposed mainly, directly or indirectly
through credit derivatives, to Investment Grade
corporate and sovereign bonds issued in the OECD.
