Objetivo de inversión
 
The investment objective of the Fund is to achieve long-
term capital appreciation. The Fund will seek to achieve
its objective over a rolling period of any five years, by
investing at least 80% in a portfolio of equity and equity-
related securities of, or relating to, companies in, or
companies that derive a substantial proportion of their
revenues from, the Asia ex-Japan region. These
companies will be listed or traded on a Regulated
Market worldwide.
