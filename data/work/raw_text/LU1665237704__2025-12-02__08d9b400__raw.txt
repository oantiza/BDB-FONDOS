Objetivo de inversión
 
The Fund has two aims to deliver a higher total return
(the combination of capital growth and income) than
that of the global equities market over any five-year
period; and to deliver an income stream that increases
every year in US Dollar terms. The Fund invests in
securities that meet the ESG Criteria, applying an
Exclusionary Approach, that are SDG-contributing with
a Positive ESG Tilt.
