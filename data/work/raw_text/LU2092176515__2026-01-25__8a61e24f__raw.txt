Objetivo de inversión
 
The Fund’s main objective is to seek to maximise total
return. The Fund invests globally at least 70% of its total
assets in the equity securities of companies whose
predominant economic activity is in the technology
sector. The remaining 30% of the total assets of the Fund
may be invested in equity securities of companies or
issuers of any size in any sector of the economy globally
and whose predominant economic activity is not in the
technology sector.
