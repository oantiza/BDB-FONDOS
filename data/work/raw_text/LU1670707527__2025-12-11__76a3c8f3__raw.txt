Objetivo de inversión
 
The Fund aims to provide a higher total return (capital
growth plus income) than that of the European equity
market over any five-year period while applying ESG
Criteria. The Fund invests at least 80% of its Net Asset
Value in the equity securities of companies across any
sector and market capitalisation that are domiciled in,
or conducting the major part of their economic activity
in Europe.
