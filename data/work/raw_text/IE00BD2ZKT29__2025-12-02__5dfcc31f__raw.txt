Objetivo de inversión
 
The investment objective of the Fund is to seek to
generate total returns through income and capital
appreciation, while limiting volatility and potential
capital losses. The Fund seeks to achieve its objective
by actively investing in a diversified range of fixed-
income and financial derivative instruments ("FDI"), a
majority (not less than 51%) issued by or referencing
emerging markets. These include corporate, sovereign
and quasi-sovereign entities.
