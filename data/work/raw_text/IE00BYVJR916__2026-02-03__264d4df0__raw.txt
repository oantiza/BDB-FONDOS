Objetivo de inversión
 
The investment objective of the fund is to seek to achieve
a total return by investing predominantly in listed
equities. The Fund will aim to achieve its investment
objective by investing not less than 70% of its Net Asset
Value directly in a diversified portfolio of equity and
equity related securities of companies engaged in
activities related to the exploration for, development of,
and production of gold and silver as well as exchange-
traded funds that reflect the movement price of gold or
silver.
