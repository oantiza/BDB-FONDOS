Objetivo de inversión
 
The fund aims to achieve capital growth over the long
term. The fund invests at least 70% of its assets, in
equities of companies that are listed, headquartered, or
do most of their business in the UK. The fund seeks to
invest in special situations equities (equities that are
undervalued and whose recovery potential is not
recognised by the market). The fund will have a mixture
of investments in larger, medium, and smaller sized
companies. The fund may also invest in money market
instruments on an ancillary basis.
