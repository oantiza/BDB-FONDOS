Objetivo de inversión
 
The investment objective of the Fund is to seek capital
appreciation through investment in equity or equity
related securities (i.e. equity warrants and convertible
bonds) of the highest quality companies listed on the
stock exchanges of the OECD countries. Investment will
be made predominantly in the equities of issuers
established in the U.S. and Western European OECD
member countries. Fund may purchase securities
denominated in any of the major convertible currencies
of the member countries of the OECD.
