Objetivo de inversión
 
The Fund’s investment objective is to increase the value
of your investment through a combination of income
and growth of capital (total return), while seeking to
reduce the effect of interest rate movements. The fund
invests at least 70% of total net assets in investment
grade corporate bonds issued anywhere in the world,
including emerging markets, and denominated in EUR.
