Objetivo de inversión
 
The Fund’s management objective is to seek to
outperform the money market interest rates slightly,
after deduction of management fees, over the
recommended term of investment. This objective will
be implemented via an active managerial approach that
promotes the sustainability of issuers through an
analysis of the ESG (environmental, social and
governance) characteristics of the securities held in the
portfolio.
