Objetivo de inversión
 
The Fund aims to provide a higher total return (capital
growth plus income) than that of the Japanese equity
market over any five-year period while applying ESG
Criteria. The Fund invests at least 80% of its Net Asset
Value in the equity securities and equity-related
instruments of companies that are domiciled in, or
conducting the major part of their economic activity in,
Japan. The Fund usually holds fewer than 60 stocks.
